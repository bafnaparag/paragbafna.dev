Frequently Asked Questions

üöÄ How do I get started with Pulse?

+

Getting started is easy:

  • Download Pulse from the App Store
  • Open the app and enter your name
  • Choose your initial mood
  • Go to the Partner tab to send an invite
  • Once your partner accepts, you'll see each other's status!

Make sure iCloud is enabled on your device for syncing to work.

üîó How do I pair with my partner?

+

To connect with your partner:

  • Go to the Partner tab in Pulse
  • Tap "Invite Partner"
  • A share sheet will open - send the link via iMessage, email, or any messaging app
  • Your partner taps the link on their iPhone/iPad
  • They'll be prompted to accept the invitation
  • Once accepted, you're connected!

Note: Both partners need iCloud enabled and an active Apple ID.

üòä How do I update my mood?

+

Updating your mood is simple:

  • Open the app and tap on your mood card in the Dashboard
  • Select from 9 expressive moods
  • Optionally add a note or use a preset phrase
  • Your partner will be notified of your update

You can also send a handwritten note by tapping the pencil icon!

‚úçÔ∏è What are handwritten notes?

+

Handwritten notes let you send personal, drawn messages:

  • Tap the pencil icon on the Dashboard
  • Draw or write with your finger or Apple Pencil
  • Choose your pen color and style
  • Tap "Send" to share with your partner
  • Your partner sees it on their Dashboard and in their widget

It's a personal way to say "I love you" or share a quick doodle! üíå

üåô What is Pause Mode?

+

Pause Mode lets you take a break without breaking the connection:

  • Tap the menu icon (‚Ä¢‚Ä¢‚Ä¢) on your status card
  • Select "Pause Updates"
  • Your partner will see "Status Paused" (neutral gray)
  • They won't see your mood changes while paused
  • Resume anytime to share updates again

Perfect for when you need alone time or are going through something difficult.

üì± How do I add the widget?

+

Add Pulse widgets to your home screen:

  • Long press on your home screen until apps wiggle
  • Tap the + button in the top corner
  • Search for "Pulse"
  • Choose from available widget sizes
  • Tap "Add Widget" and place it

The widget shows your partner's mood, note, and any handwritten messages!

+

Cycle tracking is optional and privacy-protected:

  • Enable in Settings ‚Üí Cycle Tracking
  • Set your cycle start date and average length
  • Your partner sees only translated insights (e.g., "Sensitive Phase")
  • Raw medical data is never shared
  • Sharing is opt-in and can be disabled anytime

üîß My partner's updates aren't showing

+

If syncing isn't working, try these steps:

  • Ensure both devices have iCloud enabled
  • Check that you're both signed into iCloud
  • Pull down on the Dashboard to refresh
  • Check if your partner has paused their status
  • Verify internet connection on both devices
  • Try force-quitting and reopening the app

If issues persist, try unpairing and re-inviting your partner.

üí≥ How do I restore my purchase?

+

To restore Pulse Pro:

  • Go to Settings in the app
  • Tap "Restore Purchases"
  • Sign in with the Apple ID used for purchase
  • Wait for the restoration to complete

Make sure you're using the same Apple ID that made the original purchase.

üëã How do I unpair from my partner?

+

To disconnect from your partner:

  • Go to the Partner tab
  • Tap "Unpair"
  • Confirm the action
  • Access is immediately revoked for both parties

Note: The person who sent the invite (owner) removes the share. The other person (participant) can leave the share. Your own local data remains intact.
