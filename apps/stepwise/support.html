Frequently Asked Questions

🚀 How do I create my first habit?

+

Creating a habit in Stepwise is simple:

  • Tap the + button on the main screen
  • Enter a name for your habit (e.g., "Drink Water", "Exercise")
  • Choose an icon that represents your habit
  • Select a color theme
  • Set which days of the week you want to track this habit
  • Optionally, set reminder times
  • Tap "Save" to create your habit

You can create up to 3 habits on the free tier, or unlimited habits with Premium.

✅ How do I mark a habit as complete?

+

There are several ways to complete a habit:

  • In the app: Simply tap the habit card or the checkmark button
  • From widgets: Tap the habit directly in your home screen widget
  • From notifications: Use the "Complete" action in the notification

Completed habits will show a checkmark and contribute to your streak count!

⭐ What's included in Stepwise Premium?

+

Stepwise Premium unlocks:

  • Unlimited habits (free tier is limited to 3)
  • Premium themes - Exclusive color themes and customization options
  • Advanced analytics - Detailed insights and progress charts

☁️ How does iCloud sync work?

+

iCloud sync keeps your habits synchronized across all your Apple devices:

  • Enable iCloud sync in Settings → Sync
  • Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID on all devices
  • Your habits, completions, and settings will automatically sync
  • Data is encrypted and private - only you can access it
  • Works with iPhone, iPad, and upcoming Apple Watch app

Note: iCloud sync requires an active iCloud account with available storage.

📊 How are streaks calculated?

+

Streaks track consecutive days of habit completion:

  • Complete a habit to start or continue a streak
  • Streaks only count days when the habit is scheduled
  • If you miss a scheduled day, the streak resets to 0
  • Days when a habit isn't scheduled don't break streaks
  • Your best streak is saved for motivation

Pro tip: Set realistic schedules to maintain achievable streaks!

🔔 How do I set up reminders?

+

Set reminders to never forget your habits:

  • When creating or editing a habit, toggle on "Reminders"
  • Choose specific times for each reminder
  • You can set multiple reminders per habit
  • Reminders respect your scheduled days
  • Grant notification permissions when prompted

You can also snooze reminders for 15 minutes directly from the notification.

📱 How do I add widgets to my home screen?

+

Add Stepwise widgets for quick access:

  • Long press on your home screen until apps wiggle
  • Tap the + button in the top corner
  • Search for "Stepwise"
  • Choose from small, medium, or large widget sizes
  • Place the widget on your home screen

Widgets show today's habits and allow you to mark them complete with a single tap!

🔧 My data isn't syncing between devices

+

If iCloud sync isn't working, try these steps:

  • Ensure iCloud is enabled in Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud
  • Check that Stepwise has permission to use iCloud
  • Verify you're signed into the same Apple ID on all devices
  • Check your iCloud storage isn't full
  • Try toggling iCloud sync off and on in Stepwise settings
  • Restart the app on both devices

If issues persist, contact support with your device details.

💳 I purchased Premium but it's not activated

+

To restore your Premium purchase:

  • Go to Settings → Premium
  • Tap "Restore Purchase"
  • Sign in with the Apple ID used for purchase if prompted
  • Wait for the restoration to complete

Make sure you're using the same Apple ID that made the purchase. If you continue having issues, please contact support with your purchase receipt.

📤 How do I export my data?

+

Export your habit data anytime:

  • Go to Settings → Data & Privacy
  • Tap "Export Data"
  • Choose your format (CSV or JSON)
  • Select what to export (habits, completions, or both)
  • Share or save the file using the iOS share sheet

Your data belongs to you - export it whenever you need!
